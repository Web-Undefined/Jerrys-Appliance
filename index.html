About Us

Jerry's Appliance is a small, family owned and operated appliance business in the Greeneville section of Norwich. We service and sell all major brands and types of appliances, including refurbished. Timmy's grandfather, Jerry Allain, started this business in 1972 and we have been serving New London County and beyond ever since. Our business was built on honesty, integrity, loyalty, and pride. Because we are small we can give our customers the personal service that can't be found in the larger stores, while remaining competitive on price.

Jerry's has been creating a niche in serving not only individuals, but multi-family property owners and non-profit organizations. We offer competitive pricing on new appliances along with local expertise to meet your repair needs. Please give us a call and experience the difference in working with us.
